About Me

30 October 2024 by abed karableh

I am a motivated and detail-oriented computer science graduate from Palestine Polytechnic University, with a strong foundation in software development and programming. My journey in tech has been driven by a passion for innovation and problem-solving, and I’m excited to bring my skills into real-world applications. I aim to contribute meaningfully to any project I’m part of, using my technical knowledge to make an impact.

With experience in languages like Java, Python, and C++, and skills in web development with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I enjoy tackling complex challenges and creating efficient solutions. My background in database management with MySQL and SSMS adds to my capability to build robust and scalable systems.

Relevant Skills

Programming Languages: Java, Python, C++, SQL Web Development: HTML, CSS, JavaScript Database Management: MySQL, SSMS, InfluxDB Tools and Technologies: Git, Docker, Jira Soft Skills: Problem-solving, teamwork, communication

Education

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science Palestine Polytechnic University (Graduation: June 2024)
